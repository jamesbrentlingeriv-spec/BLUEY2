SHARING

Bluey, Bingo, Bandit

"Bluey got a sparkly purple unicorn for her birthday. She loved it more than anything in the whole world!"

"'Can I have a turn?' Bingo asked. But Bluey held it tight. 'No way! It is mine!' Bingo walked away sadly."

"Bluey played alone, but it was not as fun. Bandit told her toys were always better when shared."

"She remembered Bingo sharing her crayons yesterday without even being asked. Bluey walked over to her sister."

"'Want to play unicorns? You can be the queen!' Bingo's face lit up like sunshine. 'Really?!'"

"They played the best unicorn game ever, across pillow mountains and blanket rivers. Bingo made a paper crown."

"'Thank you for sharing,' Bingo said. Sharing is not giving something away. It is making the happiness bigger."
