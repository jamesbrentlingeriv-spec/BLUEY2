TEAMWORK

Bluey, Bingo, Bandit, Chilli

"Bluey wanted to build the most amazing cubby house ever. Two rooms, a lookout tower, and a secret entrance!"

"She tried building alone, but the blanket roof would not stay up and the cushion walls kept toppling over."

"'Need some help?' Bingo asked. Bluey sighed. 'I wanted to do it myself, but nothing is working.'"

"Bingo held the blanket while Bluey tucked it over the chairs. Teamwork! The roof stayed up!"

"Bandit stacked boxes for the lookout tower. Chilli brought fairy lights. The cubby glowed like a magical castle!"

"They all squeezed inside together. 'I could not have done this alone,' Bluey admitted."

"'The best team is family,' Bandit said, squishing everyone into a group hug. Asking for help makes great things possible."
