PATIENCE

Bluey, Bingo, Bandit

"Bluey planted a seed in a little pot. She watered it carefully and placed it by the window in the sun."

"The next morning—nothing! 'Where is my flower?' she asked. 'Good things need patience,' Chilli explained."

"Bluey checked every hour. She even tried talking to the pot. Bingo sang it a growing song!"

"Days passed. Bluey got busy playing games and going on adventures. She almost forgot about her little seed."

"Then one morning, Bingo shouted, 'Bluey! Come look!' A tiny green sprout poked through the soil."

"Weeks later, a beautiful yellow sunflower opened up, as big as Bluey's face! 'I grew that!' Bluey beamed."

"'The best things in life are worth waiting for,' Bandit smiled. Bluey decided patience was not so bad after all."
