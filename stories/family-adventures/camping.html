CAMPING

Bluey, Bingo, Bandit, Chilli

The Heeler family was going camping! Bandit loaded the car with sleeping bags, a tent, and way too many snacks.

Tall trees surrounded them like friendly giants. Bingo hugged a tree and said, 'I think this tree likes me!'

Bandit tried to set up the tent, but it kept falling down. Chilli fixed it in two minutes. 'Mums know best!'

They spotted a lizard on a rock. 'What is your name, Mr. Lizard?' asked Bingo. 'I think his name is Gerald.'

That night, Bandit told funny stories around the campfire and made shadow puppets with his hands.

Bluey saw a shooting star. 'Make a wish!' Chilli whispered. Bluey wished for more adventures with her family.

They snuggled in sleeping bags as crickets chirped. 'Goodnight, family,' Bingo murmured. 'Goodnight, Bingo.'
