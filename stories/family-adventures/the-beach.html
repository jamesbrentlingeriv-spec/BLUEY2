THE BEACH

Bluey, Bingo, Bandit, Chilli

"It was a beautiful sunny day, and the Heeler family was going to the beach! Bluey and Bingo were so excited."

"They packed towels, sunscreen, and a big blue bucket. 'Last one to the water is a rotten egg!' Bluey shouted."

"Bandit pretended to run in slow motion, making silly faces. Mum set up the umbrella and smiled."

"Bluey found a hermit crab in a rock pool. 'Look, Bingo! He has a tiny house on his back!'"

"They built the biggest sandcastle ever, with towers, a moat, and a little leaf flag. 'This is the Heeler castle!'"

"A big wave washed part of it away. 'That is okay,' said Bandit. 'The best part is building it together.'"

"As the sun set, they sat eating watermelon. 'This was the best day ever,' Bluey whispered. And it truly was."
