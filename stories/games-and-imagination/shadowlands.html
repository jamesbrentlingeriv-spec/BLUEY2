"Late in the afternoon, the shadows grew long in the garden. 'Welcome to the Shadowlands!' Bluey declared."

"You could only move by stepping on shadows. If you stepped on sunny ground, you were out!"

"'There is a dragon over there!' Bluey pointed at a big tree shadow. Its branches looked like claws reaching out."

"Bingo crept along the garden bed shadow. 'I will distract the dragon!' she whispered bravely."

"Bandit got stuck—his shadow was shrinking! Bluey threw a blanket down to make a new shadow bridge. 'Rescued!'"

"They reached the Shadow Castle. 'We conquered the Shadowlands!' Bluey cheered. 'Shadow heroes!' Bingo raised her paw."

"As the sun set and shadows faded, Chilli called them for dinner. 'Same time tomorrow?' The Shadowlands would wait."
