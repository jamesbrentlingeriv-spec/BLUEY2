KEEPY UPPY

Bluey, Bingo, Bandit

"Bluey and Bingo had a balloon. The game was simple: do NOT let it touch the ground! 'This is Keepy Uppy!'"

"Bluey tapped it high. Bingo batted it with her little paws. Up, up, up it went! Then it floated toward the kitchen."

"Bandit was making a sandwich. Without even looking, he bopped it with his elbow. 'Still got it,' he said coolly."

"Chilli kicked it up with her knee while carrying laundry. 'Keepy Uppy lives!' she laughed."

"The balloon bounced off Bingo's nose, then Bluey's tail, then Dad's head! It floated toward the open window."

"Everyone gasped. Bluey made a spectacular leap and tapped it back just in time. 'SAFE!' she shouted."

"They collapsed laughing until their tummies hurt. 'Again! Again!' Bingo cheered. The best games are played together."
