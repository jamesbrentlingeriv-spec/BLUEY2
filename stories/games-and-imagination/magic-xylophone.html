MAGIC XYLOPHONE

Bluey, Bingo, Bandit, Chilli

"Bluey found a toy xylophone in the playroom. But this was no ordinary xylophone. Bluey decided it was magic!"

"'When I play a note, you have to freeze!' DING! Bandit froze like a statue, spatula in the air."

"Bingo tapped the xylophone and froze Chilli mid-yawn with her mouth wide open. They fell over laughing!"

"Then Bandit got the xylophone. He froze both girls right before they could grab their biscuits. 'Not fair, Dad!'"

"They played all morning, freezing and unfreezing each other in the silliest positions."

"Bluey learned that the real magic was not in the xylophone. It was in using your imagination to turn anything into a game!"
